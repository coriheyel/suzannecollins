Get to know Suzanne!

Suzanne Collins is an American author best known for The Hunger Games trilogy, a globally successful dystopian series that has captivated readers with its intense storytelling, social commentary, and strong characters. Born on August 10, 1962, Collins began her career as a television writer for children’s shows before transitioning to young adult fiction. Prior to The Hunger Games, she wrote The Underland Chronicles, a fantasy series inspired by Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland. Collins’ works often explore themes of war, survival, and the effects of media on society. Her writing has had a lasting impact, influencing the young adult genre and sparking discussions on power, inequality, and resistance.
